## THE EPISODE TEST

Every script must pass this:

1. **Does it open with PAST?** Land in a real moment — Place, Action, Speech, Thoughts — before any vision talk.
2. **Does it widen the gap?** Locate the viewer in the ANTI-VISION column and pull them one inch toward the VISION column.
3. **Does it show the rhythm?** Burst of work → presence in life. Both halves visible.
4. **Is AvenMaia the one acting?** She builds. Samuel & Momo are the why. Daniel makes it possible.
5. **Does it lay one brick?** One real piece of MaiaCity advanced today — a dome, a decision, a step from the tent outward.

> Every episode moves the viewer one inch from the person they are toward the Sovereign Founder they could become.
