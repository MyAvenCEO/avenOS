## WHAT WE BUILD TO PROVE IT: MaiaCity

MaiaCity is a **Spark** — the largest one. A new world order of sovereign city-states, owned and built by their citizens, the Sovereign Founders.

### The cornerstone Spark: the Dome

- 12–120 people as a self-sufficient unit. Itself a Spark.
- Geodesic dome housing inside + permaculture food forest outside.
- Grows all its own food from surrounding fields; high-yield organic permaculture, inside and out.
- Each dome is a foundational city-brick. Build enough domes and you have a city.

### The path

Samuel, Momo, and AvenMaia travel between dome-building locations — laying the literal foundation bricks of MaiaCity — living the Sovereign Founder lifestyle as they go. Valuable moments together *and* monumental, civilization-scale building. Both. Always both. From a tent, outward.
