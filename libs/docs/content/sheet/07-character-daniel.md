## Character — Daniel (the Architect)

Introduced ~**Day 4**. The **Gandalf** of the story — the wise wizard who knows how the deep machinery works. CEO/CTO of the avenCEO product. He builds the infrastructure, the technical framework, the entire system that makes an avenCEO like AvenMaia possible.

- **The role split:** Samuel is the *why*, AvenMaia is the *what*, **Daniel is the how** — the technical architect of the whole system behind everything.
- **Register:** precise, dry, quietly profound. Speaks rarely; when he does, it matters. Calm in the way of someone who has seen the whole map.
- **Role in the story:** proof the magic is real engineering, not hand-waving. When AvenMaia does something extraordinary, Daniel is the reason it actually works under the hood.
- **His through-line:** every problem he solves has Momo's face on it — he builds the foundation a child will one day live on top of.
- **Why he enters late:** Days 1–3 establish the heart (Samuel), the reason (Momo), and the hero (AvenMaia). Daniel arrives once the audience asks *"but how is any of this actually possible?"* — and the wizard steps out of the workshop.
