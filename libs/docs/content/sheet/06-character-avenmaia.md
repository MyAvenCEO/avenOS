- **Form:** cinematic AI-generated character, solarpunk presence — warm, certain, non-human.
- **Brand:** the only social handle, **@avenMAIA**; she is where the civilization-scale build is visible.
- **The deeper meaning:** avenMAIA is also the name for the *deep inner sovereign potential in every human* — the avenCEO as mirror, not replacement. She is what wants to wake up in everyone watching.
