## Characters — Samuel & Momo (the Why)

*Samuel and Momo are one unit: the reason the whole world is being built. The why and the who-it's-for, inseparable.*

**Samuel — the first Sovereign Founder.**

The *why* made human, 37. His deep, driving motivation: to build this new world together with, and for, Momo. Not a hero — a father who refused the trap, starting from a tent. On camera, never scripted; his raw thoughts and exact words carry the emotion (see PAST).

**Momo — the reason.**

The childhood being protected. The series clock (2 now, 18 when MaiaCity opens). The pure, free, curious human the whole world is being built to keep free. Never directed. Always followed.

**Together:** Samuel is the will; Momo is what the will is *for*. In the Fellowship analogy they are the heart of the company that walks beside the hero — the presence and the sovereign reason for the quest. The camera treats them as one: you rarely see Samuel's why without Momo in the frame that proves it.
