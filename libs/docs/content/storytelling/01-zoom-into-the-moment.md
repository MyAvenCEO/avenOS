## The one thing — zoom into the moment

The single most important storytelling skill is **zooming into the moment.** A war movie that opens on a helicopter shot of the whole battlefield is interesting for two seconds — then you want *down there*, in the mud, on a face. Most people tell their story from the helicopter: *"I had a vision, it was hard, I built it."* High-level. Forgettable. We always land in the trench first.

The tool for the trench is **PAST: Place, Action, Speech, Thoughts.**

> The vision is the helicopter shot. PAST is how we land in the mud first — every single episode — before we ever fly up to show the whole field.
