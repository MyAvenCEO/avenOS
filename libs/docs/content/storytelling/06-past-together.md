## Putting PAST together

A full PAST story in ~30 seconds — annotated, from the source talk:

> "I was on a plane to Russia **[P]** and the engine exploded. I'm looking out the window and the whole engine turns into a fireball **[A]**. I felt like I'd already died and gone to heaven, because nobody said anything **[T]**. I'm screaming, 'What the hell is going on?!' The stewardess says, 'We seem to have a slight problem.' The Russian guy goes, 'What's the problem?' I said, 'We lost an engine.' He goes, 'How many did we have?' 'Two. Now we have one.' **[S]**"

Four elements, thirty seconds, unforgettable.

### How we apply PAST to every episode

1. **Open on Place + time.** A real moment, never the abstract vision.
2. **Move with Action verbs** over the Samuel + Momo footage. Image and verbs are the same beat.
3. **Land the decision as Speech** — exact words, milked: anticipation → line → silence.
4. **Carry emotion through Thoughts** — Samuel's raw interior voice. AvenMaia can voice his thoughts back to surface them without it feeling staged.
5. **Only then widen out** — to the city, the 5439 days, the vision. The big scale earns its place *after* the moment hooks us, never before.
