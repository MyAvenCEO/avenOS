## T — Thoughts

Share the **raw, unfiltered thoughts** of the moment — not the named emotion, and never the polished version. Real thoughts are messy and human.

- ❌ Amateur (named emotion): "Samuel felt worried about the future."
- ❌ Polished: "I considered the systemic forces shaping my son's development."
- ✅ Ours: *"In a few years they'll put him behind a desk and train this out of him. And one day he's me — choosing every morning between the work that matters and the people who matter."*
