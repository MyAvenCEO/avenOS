## T — Thoughts

Share the **raw, unfiltered thoughts** of the moment — not the named emotion, and never the polished version. Real thoughts are messy and human. *(Samuel's voice is warm, reflective, full of quiet conviction — never panicked.)*

> **The 80/20 of Samuel's thoughts:** name the pain in a single beat to set the contrast, then spend the rest *pulling toward the future he can already see*. He is far more the visionary describing the world being built than the father escaping the old one. Pain frames; vision drives.

- ❌ Amateur (named emotion): "Samuel felt worried about the future."
- ❌ Polished: "I considered the systemic forces shaping my son's development."
- ✅ *(set the pain, briefly)* "The world will be in such a hurry to flatten that wonder out of him —"
- ✅ *(then pull to the future — this is where Samuel lives)* "— so we're building one that runs on it instead. A city where this exact curiosity is the most valuable thing a person owns. Millions of people, each one this awake. I can see it already."
- ✅ *(by the lake)* "Right now it's one tent, one dad, one kid throwing water. In 16 years it's a city of a million people who never had to choose between their work and their life. And it starts here, on this patch of grass, today."
- ✅ *(AvenMaia, voicing his thought back)* "And you're thinking — *this little square of turned soil is the first square metre of the whole city. I'm not imagining it anymore. I'm standing in it.*"
