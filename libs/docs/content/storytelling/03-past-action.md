## A — Action

Start *in* the action with forward momentum — no context dump first. Drive it with concrete present-tense **action verbs**; each one plays a movie in the viewer's mind.

- ❌ Helicopter: "We were spending quality time together that afternoon."
- ✅ *"Momo grabs his little watering can, dumps the whole thing on my leg, fills it from the lake, dumps it again. An hour vanishes."*
- ✅ *"We kneel by the new bed, I press a seedling into the soil, and Momo immediately pulls it back out, holding it up to show me, beaming."*
- ✅ *"At the backyard table I open the laptop, give Maia the morning's three decisions, close it again — and I'm already counting the hours to the train."*
- ✅ *"Momo wades into the lake up to his knees, shrieks at the cold, turns around to check I'm still watching."*
