## P — Place

State *where and when*, early and plainly. Not a scenic postcard — just enough that the listener believes it's true. **Always anchor a date + location**; the instant you do, people accept it as a real story.

- ❌ Helicopter: "Samuel had a vision for a new kind of city."
- ✅ Ours: *"Last Tuesday. A campsite outside Munich. Inside a small dome tent, rain on the canvas."*
