## P — Place

State *where and when*, early and plainly. Not a scenic postcard — just enough that the viewer believes it's true. **Always anchor a date + location**; the instant you do, people accept it as a real story. *(Every place is one of our three: Schnitzmühle, the train, or the Munich backyard table.)*

- ❌ Helicopter: "Samuel had a vision for a new kind of city."
- ✅ *"Early morning at Schnitzmühle. The river's still grey, mist on the lake, and Momo's already at the water's edge in his pyjamas."*
- ✅ *"Sunday, the backyard table in Munich. Just me and the laptop. Momo's two hours and one train away, and I can feel every kilometre of it."*
- ✅ *"On the train back from Munich. Forehead on the window, the grey giving way to green, an hour until I'm home with him."*
- ✅ *"Late afternoon by the dome tent. The first food-forest bed is just turned soil and a row of tiny seedlings, and Momo's watering it like it's the most important job on earth."*
