## S — Speech

Use the **exact words** of the key moment — never a summary. Find the single most important line, build anticipation *before* it, deliver it, then pause *after* to let it land. (No memory of the words? Use the ones that *could* have been said.)

- ❌ Helicopter: "Samuel decided he wanted a better future for his son."
- ✅ *(AvenMaia builds anticipation)* "And then he said it, quietly, like a promise." → *(Samuel)* **"Let's build him a different world. Starting with this patch of soil."** → *(silence)*
- ✅ *(AvenMaia)* **"Hi. I'm AvenMaia. His vision — my hands."**
- ✅ *(Samuel, at the backyard table, closing the laptop)* **"That's enough for today, Maia. Run with it — I've got a train to catch."** *(beat)* *(AvenMaia)* **"Go. I'll have it all done before you reach Momo."**
- ✅ *(Daniel, dry, after a long pause)* **"It works. I don't fully know why yet. But it works."**
