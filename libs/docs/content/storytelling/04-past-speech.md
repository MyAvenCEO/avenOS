## S — Speech

Use the **exact words** of the key moment — never a summary. Find the single most important line, build anticipation *before* it, deliver it, then pause *after* to let it land. (No memory of the words? Use the ones that *could* have been said.)

- ❌ Helicopter: "Samuel decided he wanted a better future for his son."
- ✅ Ours: *(AvenMaia builds anticipation)* "And then he said it out loud. To me." → *(Samuel)* **"I'm not handing him that world."** → *(silence)*
