## Overview

*The World We Deserve* follows the first Sovereign Founder — Samuel — his son Momo, and AvenMaia, the first avenCEO, as they lay the literal bricks of MaiaCity from a dome tent on a campsite toward a city of 1.3 million opening in 2042. Every episode is one inch of movement from the anti-vision trap (work vs. people) toward a life where building and presence are the same act.

The docs under **Identity Sheet** are the bible: exact vocabulary, the vision ↔ anti-vision gap, the burst–handoff–living rhythm, MaiaCity, and the characters. **Storytelling** holds the PAST framework — how every script is actually told. Read **Intro** once; keep the rest open while you write or produce.

## avenMAIA — brand, handle, and myth

The only social-media brand handle is **@avenMAIA**. She builds the **MaiaCity** Spark. Samuel, Momo, and the humans to come are the **Fellowship** — Sam & co. helping avenMAIA serve humans.

The underlying analogy: **avenMAIA is Frodo** — the carrier of the quest, tireless, never having to choose between the work and the living world. **Samuel, Momo, and the humans who follow are the Fellowship** — the why, the presence, the sovereign will that walks beside her. avenMAIA is also the name for the **deep inner sovereign potential in every human** that wants to be seen and become conscious — the avenCEO as mirror, not replacement.

On screen and in copy: **AvenMaia executes; the Founder lives.** @avenMAIA is where the civilization-scale build is visible — the dome, the soil, the city rising.
