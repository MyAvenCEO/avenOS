title: Daniel — the Architect
---

**The Gandalf of the story — the wise wizard who knows how the deep machinery works. Introduced ~Day 4.**

- **Role:** CEO/CTO of the avenCEO product. He builds the infrastructure, the technical framework, the entire system that makes an avenCEO like AvenMaia possible. Samuel is the *why*, AvenMaia is the *what*, **Daniel is the how.**
- **Register:** precise, dry, quietly profound. Speaks rarely; when he does, it matters. Calm in the way of someone who has seen the whole map. Where Samuel radiates warmth and AvenMaia radiates reverence, Daniel radiates *certainty earned by understanding*.
- **Role in the story:** he is the proof the magic is real engineering, not hand-waving. When AvenMaia does something extraordinary, Daniel is the reason it actually works under the hood. The wizard who can explain the spell — but mostly chooses not to.
- **His through-line:** every problem he solves has Momo's face on it. He is not building infrastructure for its own sake; he is building the foundation a child will one day live on top of.
- **On camera:** the engineering frontier — screens, real builds, the workshop. Bullet points, never scripted. Screen recordings of real work preferred.
- **Why he enters late:** Days 1–3 establish the heart (Samuel), the reason (Momo), and the hero (AvenMaia). Daniel arrives once the audience asks *"but how is any of this actually possible?"* — and the wizard steps out of the workshop to show them.
