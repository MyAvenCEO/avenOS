## Vocabulary — get this exact

- **Sovereign Founder** = the *person*. The new breed of human who owns every aspect of their life — time, vision, food, money, presence. Co-creates Sparks with their avenCEO.
- **avenCEO** = the *agentic AI partner*. Executes and creates the value. AvenMaia is the first.
- **Spark** = the *organizational unit* a Sovereign Founder co-creates with an Aven. A coop, a DAO, a company, a dome — even MaiaCity itself. Sparks are things you *build*, not people.
- **Dome** = the cornerstone Spark. A self-sufficient living unit (12–120 people). Also the literal first brick of MaiaCity.

> Clean sentence: *A Sovereign Founder, paired with their avenCEO, builds Sparks. MaiaCity is a Spark. An avenCEO company is a Spark. A dome is a Spark.*

## The one-line identity

> A Sovereign Founder owns every aspect of their life — pairs with an avenCEO to build civilization-scale Sparks in short intense bursts — and spends the rest of their hours fully present, in flow, with the people and the living world they're building for.

This is the person every viewer is being invited to become. Every episode measures the distance between where they are now and this.
