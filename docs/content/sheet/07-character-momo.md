---
title: Momo — the Reason
---

**The two-year-old. The reason the whole world is being built — and the clock that measures it.**

- **Age / role:** 2 now, 18 when MaiaCity opens in 2042. His age is literally the series timeline: every episode, he is a little older, and the city is a little more real.
- **What he represents:** the pure, free, curious human the entire world is being built to keep free. His wonder, spontaneity, and flow are the highest virtues of the vision — alive, in front of the camera, every episode.
- **On camera:** never directed, never miked, always followed. We never stage him. We roll and we wait. He pours water, wades into the lake, pulls the seedling back out to show us. He is the living proof of the thesis — you do not have to choose between building a world and being present for *this*.
- **The camera belongs to him:** the visual language of the series is shot from Momo's eye level (see Production). We are pulled into his world, and Samuel comes down to his height.
- **What he is NOT:** a prop, a punchline, a performer. He is the sovereign reason for the quest.
