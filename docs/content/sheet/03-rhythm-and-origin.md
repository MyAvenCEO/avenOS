## The pairing — the new breed of founder

The Sovereign Founder is one half of a pair:

- **The Sovereign Founder (human):** creative vision, intention, taste, the *why*. Sparkling eyes, fire in the belly.
- **The avenCEO (agentic AI):** executes, builds, creates the value. Tireless. Never sleeps. Never has to choose.

This pairing is the *only* reason the lifestyle below is possible. It's what lets one man in a dome tent on a campsite begin building a city.

## The lifestyle we always show

The rhythm of a Sovereign Founder's day — **time-conscious by design**:

1. **The Burst** — short, intense, fully focused session *with* the avenCEO. Vision in, direction set, decisions made. 60–90 minutes of total fire.
2. **The Handoff** — the Founder lets the avenCEO run. The work continues without them.
3. **The Living** — the Founder is *gone* into life: hands in the soil of the food forest, in the dome, on the ground with their child, in the moment. Fully present, screen down.

> The avenCEO works while the Founder lives. That contrast — burst, then presence — is the show. We cut between them constantly.

**Contrast this, every time, with the Western default:** the 9-to-5 that bleeds into 9-to-9. Notifications at dinner. The parent on the laptop while the kid plays alone. The weekend spent recovering from the week. *We show the Founder's rhythm against this ghost — always.*

## The origin — a dome tent on a campsite

We do not start in a finished city. We start at the humblest possible seed:

> **Samuel and Momo, in a small dome tent on a campsite.** The first Sovereign Founder. The first dome. The first brick of MaiaCity.

The whole epic — a city of 1.3 million, opening 2042 — begins with a father, a toddler, a tent, and an AI. From a dome *tent* to a domed *city*. That arc is the series. The tent is Momo's-age clock made architectural: a tent now, a real geodesic dome in a decade, a city of them in 2042.
